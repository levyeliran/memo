V0.2

This project is designed to help users carry out the process of photos sharing experience in a simple and easy way, with the possibility of photos sharing between relatives and maintaining privacy.

That is, each user can set an "Event" in the app, which behind the scenes allows him to manage a folder of photos on the server. The user can invite relatives or friends to upload photos that they take and edit, as well tag photos between members of the event.

While do so, the application produces animation from the photos uploaded by users.
